# Quick Start: Vendor Messaging System

## For Business Owners

### Step 1: Add a Public Vendor
1. Go to **Dashboard → Vendors**
2. Browse the **Marketplace** tab
3. Click "Add to My Vendors" on any public vendor

### Step 2: Start Messaging
1. Switch to **My Vendors** tab
2. Find the public vendor you added
3. Click **"Message Vendor"** button
4. Start chatting instantly!

### Step 3: Access Your Inbox
- Click **Messages** in the sidebar navigation
- See all your conversations with vendors
- Unread messages are highlighted with a badge

## For Public Vendors

### Receiving Messages
1. Clients will appear in your **Messages** inbox when they contact you
2. You'll receive an email notification if you're offline
3. Unread count shows on each conversation

### Responding
1. Click on any conversation to open it
2. Type your reply and press Enter
3. Client receives your message instantly

- **Enter** - Send message
- **Shift + Enter** - New line in message

## Tips

1. **Stay Connected**: Keep the Messages tab open for instant notifications
2. **Quick Replies**: Use the message button on vendor cards for fast access
3. **Check Regularly**: Visit your inbox to stay updated on vendor communications
4. **Professional**: Keep messages business-focused and professional

## Troubleshooting

**Messages not appearing?**
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure the vendor is a "Public" vendor

**Not receiving emails?**
- Check spam folder
- Verify your email address in settings

**Can't find a vendor?**
- Only public vendors support messaging
- Private/Virtual vendors use traditional contact methods
